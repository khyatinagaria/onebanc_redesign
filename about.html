About Us

We are a neo-bank, building the economic infrastructure for the Indian workforce. The idea was conceived when a young girl asked Vibhore, a serial entrepreneur, why the money in her piggy bank never grew. Adopting this philosophy of #DemandMore, we connect enterprises, banks, and HR Tech platforms to enhance value for all stakeholders. Core team members have proven their vision and executive skills in CoCubes, a complete assessment solution for students and institutes, acquired by Aon. Now they are building the squad to revolutionize banking.
